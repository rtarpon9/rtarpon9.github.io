About Pawfect Match

Pawfect Match 🐾 is a fictional pet adoption website built for our final lab exam in the Object-Oriented Programming (OBJPROG) course. This site demonstrates our skills in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and basic front-end web development.

🎯 Our Goal

Our mission is to provide a friendly and responsive platform for users to explore adoptable pets. The site is tailored toward animal lovers, students, and families who want to virtually experience the process of adoption.

This website is purely for educational purposes, but it reflects the importance of animal welfare and promotes the idea of giving animals a second chance.
