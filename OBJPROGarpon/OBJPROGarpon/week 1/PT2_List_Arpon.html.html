Scramble eggs

eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scramble eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs off butter
  • 2 tbs cream

    • Methods

    1. Melt the butter in frying pan over medium heat
    2. gently mix the egg and cream in a bowl
    3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
      • Use premium cream
      • Use duck eggs
    4. Using as spatula fold the egg from the edge off the pan to the center every 20 seconds
    5. When egg are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
